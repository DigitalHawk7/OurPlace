A Little About Us

Being a family owned company you would think it would be hard to maintain a professional appearance and approach to the way Our Place is run. In all reality it makes it easier because we can always be honest with each other and we know all of our knicks and knacks of each other. We have always wanted to own our own restaurant and when we saw the opportunity in small town Stanhope, Iowa we jumped at the chance to show our strengths and what we can do to help the town.

As advocates of small town businesses, because we are a small town business ourselves, we have taken it upon ourselves to help promote the amazing meat locker right next door. All our red meats come from the local Stanhope Meat Locker and are always fresh. We pride ourselves in customer statisfaction from drinks and food served to conversations with our staff if an ear is needed.

A little about the structure: All the wood that you see when entering Our Place is handmade by someone in our family from real cedar.
